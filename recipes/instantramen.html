Instant Ramen Noodles

We all love a quick lunch or even a quick snack. And we all love noodles. You cannot go wrong with instant ramen noodles. I love the spicy ones and I even like to add extra ingredients and seasoning to it to make it my own. My secret ingredient is Sesame oil.

Ingredients and Seasoning

  1. Instant Ramen
  2. Garlic
  3. Sesame oil
  4. Brown Sugar
  5. Light Soy Sauce
  6. Egg
  7. Spring onion
  8. 1 1/2 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
  9. 1 teaspoon coarse salt
  10. 1/2 tablespoon Chilli flakes

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles to al dente. You don't want the noodles too mushy! If you like your noodles with a firm, chewy bite, consider undercooking by 1 minute.
  2. Make the sauce. Mix the sesame oil, and saute the garlic and red pepper flakes. Mix in the brown sugar and soy sauce and stir until dissolved.
  3. Mix the sesame seeds, salt and chilli flakes in a bowl.
  4. Toss the noodles and sauce together until completely coated. You can do this right in the pan.
  5. Scramble the egg. Make space in the same pan (no need to remove the noodles) for the egg and cook until fluffy. Then, stir it into the noodles. Or, if its easier, cook the eggs in a separate pan and add them just before serving.
  6. Sprinkle the seasoning on top and some freshly cut spring onions. Enjoy!
